Portfolio Review: Grant Miller

Hello, and welcome! The purpose of this three-page portfolio website is to showcase my progress and prowess while learning at Brigham Young University-Idaho. My name is Grant Miller, and I am studying to be a Graphic Designer with an emphasis in UX/UI, or user experience and interface, and I am also minoring in Web Design. Essentially user experience and interface refers to the human interaction between product or application, and the quality therein. I have always enjoyed finding the balance in beautiful form and function, and pursuing a degree in Graphic Design has allowed me to learn and express those very techniques.
